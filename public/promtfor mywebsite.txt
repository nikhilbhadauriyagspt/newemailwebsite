Section 6: FAQ
Heading:
Frequently Asked Questions

Use accordion style UI with plus icon.

FAQs:
1. Why am I not receiving emails?
Answer: It may happen due to storage limits, spam filters, sync issues, blocked senders, or incorrect settings.

2. Why are my emails going to spam?
Answer: Filters, sender reputation, mailbox rules, or message content can move emails to spam.

3. Why can’t I log in to my email?
Answer: Password changes, account locks, verification issues, or suspicious activity alerts can cause login problems.

4. Why are attachments not opening?
Answer: The file may be too large, unsupported, blocked, or your browser or app may need updating.

5. Why is email loading slow?
Answer: Large inbox size, slow internet, outdated apps, or too many browser extensions can cause slow loading.

6. How can I secure my email account?
Answer: Use a strong password, update recovery details, review login activity, and avoid suspicious links.
